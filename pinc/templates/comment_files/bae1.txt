<p>BAE projects have two special rules that always apply, <i>whether
or not</i> the general Guidelines currently say the same thing:<br>
* Preserve page numbers in the index, table of contents, etc.<br>
* Preserve all information about diacritical marks, sub-/superscripts and other special characters.</p>

<p>BAE-specific information is collected in the
<a href = "http://www.pgdp.net/wiki/BAE/Formatting#Proofing">BAE Proofreading Wiki</a>.
For everything else, refer to the
<a href = "http://www.pgdp.net/c/faq/proofreading_guidelines.php">Proofreading Guidelines</a>
and <a href="http://www.pgdp.net/c/faq/document.php">Formatting Guidelines</a>.</p>

<p>For more information about BAE projects, see
<a href = "http://www.pgdp.net/phpBB3/viewtopic.php?p=50987&start=2">the Uber-Project
thread</a>.</p>
